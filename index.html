Smart Cooler Technology

Built Different.
Powered by AI.

Our coolers go far beyond traditional vending — AI cameras, cashless payments, and dual-climate zones make every purchase seamless.

AI-Powered Auto-Charging

Built-in cameras recognize exactly what's taken or returned and automatically charge users — no scanning required.

Tap or Swipe to Open

Completely cashless and contactless. Users pay with any card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or mobile app.

Dual-Climate Zones

Right side refrigerated for drinks and cold snacks. Left side ambient temperature for chips, candy, and dry goods.

Locked & Secure

Doors remain locked at all times. Only paying users can access the cooler, keeping inventory fully secure.

Custom Inventory

We work with your community to stock what residents actually want — local favorites, healthy options, energy drinks, and more.
